Historical Landmarks

Guangdong's historical landmarks tell the story of over two millennia of Chinese civilization. As the gateway to the Maritime Silk Road, the province has been a crossroads of cultures, religions, and ideas since the Qin Dynasty established its southern administrative center here in 214 BC.

Each monument, temple, and colonial building reveals layers of history — from the elegant wooden carvings of ancestral halls to the grand European-style architecture of the treaty port era. The architectural legacy of Guangdong spans traditional Lingnan style, international colonial influences, and bold contemporary design, creating a unique urban landscape found nowhere else in the world.

Iconic Landmarks of Guangdong

Chen Clan Ancestral Hall

Built in 1894 during the late Qing Dynasty, the Chen Clan Ancestral Hall is widely regarded as the finest example of traditional Lingnan architecture in existence. Originally constructed as a joint ancestral temple and academy for Chen family scholars from across Guangdong, this magnificent complex features elaborate wood carvings, stone engravings, brick sculptures, pottery figurines, and iron castings that represent the pinnacle of Cantonese folk art.

Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall

The Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall, constructed in 1931, stands as a tribute to Dr. Sun Yat-sen, the founding father of modern China and a native of Zhongshan, Guangdong. This octagonal building, designed by the renowned architect Lu Yanzhi, combines traditional Chinese architectural elements with modern construction techniques. Its distinctive blue glazed tile roof and reinforced concrete octagonal structure were revolutionary for their time.

Canton Tower

Rising to a height of 600 meters, the Canton Tower is the tallest structure in Guangzhou and an iconic symbol of modern China. Designed by the Dutch architects Mark Hemel and Barbara Kuit, its revolutionary twisted hyperboloid structure has earned it the nickname "Super Model" among locals. The tower's sleek, organic form was inspired by the graceful curve of a woman's waist, and its luminous LED lighting system transforms it into a dazzling spectacle each evening.
